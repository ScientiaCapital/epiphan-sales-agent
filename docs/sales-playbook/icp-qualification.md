# ICP Qualification

Plain English guide to what lead scores mean and how to act on them.

---

## What Is ICP Scoring?

**ICP = Ideal Customer Profile**

We score every lead from 0-100 based on how well they match our best customers. Higher score = better fit = more likely to buy.

---

## The 4 Tiers

| Tier | Score | What It Means | What You Do |
|------|-------|---------------|-------------|
| **Tier 1** | 70+ | Hot lead, matches ICP well | Call immediately, involve AE early |
| **Tier 2** | 50-69 | Good lead, decent fit | Standard outreach sequence |
| **Tier 3** | 30-49 | Lower priority | Add to marketing nurture |
| **Not ICP** | <30 | Poor fit | Don't waste time, disqualify |

---

## The 5 Scoring Dimensions

We look at 5 things to determine the score:

### 1. Company Size (25% of score)

**Why it matters:** Bigger companies = more rooms = bigger deals

| Size | Employees | Score Contribution |
|------|-----------|-------------------|
| Enterprise | 1,000+ | 25 points |
| Mid-market | 100-999 | 20 points |
| Small business | 10-99 | 10 points |
| Too small | <10 | 0 points |

**Example:** A university with 15,000 employees gets the full 25 points.

---

### 2. Industry Vertical (20% of score)

**Why it matters:** Some industries buy from us more often

| Industry | Score Contribution |
|----------|-------------------|
| Higher Education | 20 points |
| Healthcare | 18 points |
| Corporate/Enterprise | 16 points |
| Broadcast/Media | 14 points |
| Legal/Government | 12 points |
| Other | 6 points |

**Example:** A hospital gets 18 points. A random tech startup gets 6 points.

---

### 3. Use Case Fit (25% of score)

**Why it matters:** Do they actually need what we sell?

| Use Case | Score Contribution |
|----------|-------------------|
| Live streaming (AV Directors, Tech Directors) | 25 points |
| Lecture capture (L&D, Training, Simulation) | 22.5 points |
| Recording only (Courts, EHS, Legal) | 15 points |
| Consumer/Student | 0 points |

**Example:** An AV Director at a university (live streaming) gets 25 points. A student intern gets 0.

---

### 4. Tech Stack Signals (15% of score)

**Why it matters:** If they use competitor products, they're already in the market

| Signal | Score Contribution |
|--------|-------------------|
| Uses competitor (Panopto, Kaltura, Vaddio, etc.) | 15 points |
| Has LMS/collaboration tools (Canvas, Teams) | 12 points |
| No relevant tech detected | 7.5 points |

**Example:** A company using Panopto gets 15 points (replacement opportunity).

---

### 5. Buying Authority (15% of score)

**Why it matters:** Can they actually make the purchase decision?

| Role | Score Contribution |
|------|-------------------|
| Budget holder (Director, VP, C-level) | 15 points |
| Influencer (Manager, Senior) | 10.5 points |
| End user (Specialist, Analyst) | 6 points |
| Student/Intern | 0 points |

**Example:** An AV Director gets 15 points. An IT Analyst gets 6 points.

## How to Use This Information

### When You Get a Tier 1 Lead

1. Call within 5 minutes (9x higher conversion)
2. Use the appropriate persona script
3. Get AE involved early
4. Track in CRM as high priority

### When You Get a Tier 2 Lead

1. Add to standard outreach sequence
2. Personalize based on persona
3. Follow up consistently
4. Move to Tier 1 if they show high intent

### When You Get a Tier 3 Lead

1. Add to marketing nurture
2. Don't spend significant time
3. Check back if they take high-intent actions
4. May upgrade to Tier 2 over time

### When You Get a Not ICP Lead

1. Don't call
2. Mark as disqualified in CRM
3. Move on to higher-value leads

---

## Missing Information

Sometimes we don't have complete data. The system will tell you what's missing:

| Missing Data | Impact |
|--------------|--------|
| Employee count | Company Size = 0 |
| Industry | Defaults to "Other" = 6 points |
| Title | May affect Use Case and Authority scores |
| Tech stack | Defaults to "No solution" = 7.5 points |

If missing info drops a lead to a lower tier than expected, try to enrich the data before giving up.
